At Home Exercise

This is a personal at home exercise page, written by someone who is sendentary by nature, with no formal training or expertise. My personal goal is to stay uninjured, active and mobile at least until I am 100 years old. For about 25 years, I worked out at a gym (off and on), took pilates, yoga and other classes and had many sessions with trainers. Now I work out entirely at home. My biggest online influences are the Tapp Brothers and GMB. Even for free, both organizations are so helpful that I want to give them money.
