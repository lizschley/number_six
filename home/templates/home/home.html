Elizabeth Schley's Personal Study and Fun Site

This site is all about learning and sharing information. Although I have many ideas for the future, for now it is a portfolio and study aid.

When I am an employed programmer, work always comes first. Yet, I always imagine applications that I would create, if my priorities were different. This site will eventually give me the platform to do that. But in the here and now, my first goal is to create a site, allowing me to learn whatever I want, whenever I want, and to share the information with anyone interested.

Note about home page images


My Personal Requirements:

  1. No logins
  2. No typing https to get https and no www ever (it won't work)
  3. No extra buttons when a refresh or back button will work just as well.
  4. No data entry using forms
  5. Only a few static pages. Most pages will pull the data from the database, allowing the same information to be displayed on its own page, as a popup or as an answer to the a flashcard question
  6. Having a flexible format, so that different pages can be created by simply loading data with different categories and groups
  7. Easy and convenient to upload data and static files
  8. Excellent automated tests (the only requirement I have not met to my own satisfation)
