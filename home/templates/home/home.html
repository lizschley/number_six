Study and Fun Site

This site is all about learning and sharing information. Although I have many ideas for the future, for now it is a portfolio and study aid.

When I am an employed programmer, work always comes first. Yet, I always imagine applications to create. This site will eventually give me the platform to do that. But in the here and now, my first goal is to create a site, helping me learn whatever I want, whenever I want, and to share the information with anyone interested.

Note about home page images


Decisions, based partially on this being a personal site:

  1. No logins
  2. Automatic Https
  3. No extra buttons when a refresh or back button will work just as well
  4. No database updates using a form as input
  5. Data driven display: most pages will pull the data from the database, allowing the same information to be displayed on a page in many ways, based on the category, group and/or paragraph attributes. For example, the same paragraph can have a page or pop-up of its own, or can be part of an ordered group, such as an answer to a flashcard question or part of step by step instructions
  6. Easy and convenient to upload data and static files
  7. Excellent automated tests (the unmet requirement)
